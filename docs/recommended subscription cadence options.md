# Recommended Subscription Cadence Options

## Research-based guidance

There does not appear to be strong research showing a clear universal winner between a **quarterly** plan and a **semi-annual** plan as the third SaaS subscription option.

What current pricing research supports more strongly is:

- **monthly + annual** is the dominant SaaS pattern
- **annual** tends to improve retention and cash flow
- intermediate cadences are less standard and much less studied

I did not find solid benchmark research saying that **quarterly clearly outperforms semi-annual**, or vice versa. Most available pricing reports and guides focus on **monthly vs annual**, and treat other cadences as secondary or less common.

## Practical recommendation for this app

For this app, the best starting point is likely **monthly + yearly only**, not three billing cadences.

This product appears closer to a self-serve habit/productivity SaaS than to a contract-heavy B2B product, so the usual best default is:

- **monthly** for lower-friction adoption
- **yearly** for stronger commitment, better retention, and better cash flow

Adding a third billing cadence at launch would likely add more complexity than value:

- more pricing-page decision friction
- more Stripe/config/documentation/testing surface area
- more risk of cannibalizing annual without a large upside

## If a third option is needed

If a third billing cadence is later justified, **quarterly** is the better default choice over semi-annual.

Reasons to prefer quarterly:

- it is a more familiar offer
- it is a smaller commitment step up from monthly
- it is easier to explain and compare
- it feels less unusual than a 6-month plan in a self-serve SaaS

Reasons semi-annual may be weaker:

- it is less common
- it can feel arbitrary
- it may add complexity without being as compelling as annual
- it can cannibalize annual without delivering the full annual retention and cash-flow benefits

## Bottom line

- There is **not** strong research proving that quarterly beats semi-annual.
- If a middle option is wanted, **quarterly** is the safer default.
- For this app, the recommended launch packaging is **monthly + yearly**.
- If a 3-option structure is later needed, the recommended order is **monthly + quarterly + yearly**.
- The least preferred option is **monthly + semi-annual + yearly**.

## Simple decision rule

- If there is no real customer data yet, launch with **2 options**.
- If users resist annual but monthly churn is too high, test **quarterly**.
- Only use **semi-annual** if there is a specific business reason to do so.
